## Recommendation

Scenario A (Retrofit) dominates Scenario B (New Install) on both primary metrics at identical confidence: A delivers a likely ROI of 2.583 versus B's 2.087, while costing $189,704 less ($1,181,413 vs $1,371,117) over three years. Both branches carry 4 flagged assumptions of equivalent severity — including the same fabricated 35% maintenance savings claim and the same unrealistically low $12,000 inference budget — meaning the higher-ROI branch does not carry proportionally more risk. The overall confidence for both branches is 55%, providing no statistical basis to prefer B's lower return. Scenario A's payback of 3.8 months is also slightly faster than B's 4.0 months. Recommendation is qualified by the shared 55% overall confidence across both branches, which reflects that all 4 flagged assumptions remain unvalidated in each scenario.
