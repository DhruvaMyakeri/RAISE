## Decision Framing

Meridian Retail Co. is evaluating Meridian Assist: Deploy an AI Tier-1 support assistant to deflect routine tickets (order status, returns eligibility, password resets) before they reach human agents. The hosting architecture has not been finalized. This memo presents two scenarios — Scenario A — On-prem and Scenario B — Cloud — each modeled across conservative, likely, and optimistic financial assumptions. All ROI numbers are computed by a deterministic modeling tool; no LLM-generated arithmetic.

## Recommendation

Scenario B (Cloud) is recommended. It delivers superior ROI (6.61x vs 5.69x likely case) at a lower 3-year cost ($1.14M vs $1.29M), a savings of ~$157K. Both scenarios share the same core flagged assumptions (deflection math inconsistency, inference budget understatement), but Scenario B carries one additional flag (ROI exceeds realistic ceiling of 5.8x). However, this incremental risk is outweighed by the concrete cost advantage and higher ROI—both of which are measurable and certain. Scenario B's overall confidence is 51%, acknowledging that both branches rely heavily on unvalidated assumptions that should be verified before final investment. Recommendation confidence is limited to 51% (overall confidence for Scenario B) due to shared unvalidated assumptions across both branches, including deflection rate math and inference budget estimates that contradict company documentation and benchmark frameworks.
